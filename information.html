The Intracacies of Adoption

Adopting animals can be an overwhelming process. Here are some good tips and information to make your process less stressful!

The Process Overview

Starting with how to adopt, find a shelter near you, make sure you do research or use our site to confirm that they’re a good place to adopt from and have what you're looking for.



Cost

The cost of adoption can depend on the animal's breed, age, health, and the organisation you're buying from. For example, for dogs, purebreds and puppies usually cost more since they are more expensive to take care of, so if you are looking for a cheaper option, buying an older pet may be more up your alley. A typical average price range is from around 50-500 dollars, which does not include toys, pet furniture, food, etc.



Love and Care

Caring for pets coming out of a shelter can be a tough process, depending on where they came from. Many animals in shelters come from not-so-great decisions, so it may be a while before they feel entirely comfortable around you. The best thing you can do is be patient and let them explore your home at their own pace and open up to you when they’re ready. Making sure their area is comfortable and safe with plenty of food and water will surely heal their hearts.
